This Privacy Policy explains how OhMochi (“we,” “our,” or “us”) handles information when you use the mobile application TradeCheck Pro (“App”).

By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

TradeCheck Pro does not collect any personal or sensitive information.

We only process limited technical and subscription-related data:

  • App User ID – generated automatically to identify your device session.
  • Subscription and purchase status – used to manage premium features through RevenueCat.
  • Advertising data – automatically collected by Unity Ads to show ads and measure ad performance.

No email, password, contact list, or precise location is collected.

2. How Data Is Used

  • RevenueCat uses subscription data to confirm active plans and manage purchases.
  • Unity Ads uses anonymous data to deliver personalized or contextual ads.
  • We use these services only to keep the App working and free for basic users.
  • We do not sell or share user data with any other party.

3. Data Storage and Processing

All data is stored and processed by the third-party providers listed above:

  • RevenueCat, Inc. – for managing in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Unity Technologies – for ad delivery and analytics.

These providers may store data on servers located outside your country.

Each follows its own privacy policy and security standards.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party service providers, as they are not governed by this Privacy Policy:

  • Google Play Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Unity Game Player and App User Privacy Policy

4. Children’s Privacy

TradeCheck Pro is not intended for users under 18 years old (or the minimum age allowed in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

5. Data Retention

We keep subscription and transaction records only as long as needed for purchase verification and legal or accounting requirements. Ad data is managed by Unity Ads according to their retention policy.

6. Your Choices

  • You can disable personalized ads in your device settings.
  • You can delete the App at any time to stop data collection.
  • If you request data deletion related to RevenueCat purchases, email us and we will forward the request to RevenueCat.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if we add new features or third-party services. The latest version will always be available in the App or on our website.
