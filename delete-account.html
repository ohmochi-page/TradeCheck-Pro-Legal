Note: TradeCheck Pro uses Google Account for subscriptions. We do not delete your Google Account. This only applies to data stored by TradeCheck Pro.

Steps to Delete Your Data

  1. Cancel subscription via Google Play Store → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → TradeCheck Pro → Cancel subscription.
  2. Cancel subscription via web browser by clicking this link: https://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions
  3. Request deletion by emailing ohmochisukabumi@gmail.com with your TradeCheck Pro account email.

Data Deleted

  • App preferences
  • Subscription records in RevenueCat
  • Usage analytics

Data Not Deleted

  • Google Account data
  • Google transaction logs
  • Data required by law

Retention

  • Deletion processed within 30 days
  • Confirmation email sent after completion
